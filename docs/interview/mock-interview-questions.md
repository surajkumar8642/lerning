## 29. Speaking Rule for Every Answer

Keep this pattern:

1. project
2. purpose
3. role
4. your work
5. impact

## 30. Final Reminder

Do not start with a long list of technologies.

Start with:

- what the project is
- what it does
- what your role was
- what you did
- what changed because of your work
