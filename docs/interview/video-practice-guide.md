## 19. Vocal Practice Tips

- Speak 10 to 15 percent slower than normal.
- Stress words like `architecture`, `scalable`, `integration`, `performance`, `security`, and `delivery`.
- Pause after project names.
- End sentences firmly.
- Do not say too many filler words like `actually`, `basically`, or `only`.
- Smile slightly when saying your introduction.
